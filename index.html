About Us

Eastern Aluminium Extrusion was formed in 2013.

EAEF was formerly known as a TECALUM Arabia, the company within a time period spanning less than 5 years has become one of the largest manufacturer, stockholder and distributor of ALUMINUM and other semi-finished nonferrous products in the Eastern Province.

EAEF capabilities include one of the largest extrusion facilities located at its manufacturing and operations facility in the third industrial city, Dammam. In addition, extrusion manufacturing facilities are also located in Dammam Second Industrial City, all supplying world class products at short lead times.

Our Mission
To be the most recognized brand in Aluminum Extrusion within the GCC and beyond, while creating value for our shareholders, employees and customers.

Powder Coating

EAEF has state-of-the-art powder coating lines which are equipped to handle profiles of various dimensions. Powder coatings offered are thermosetting powders which provide excellent performance with a wide choice of colours.

Our coatings are available for all profiles be it for facades, high rises or window sections for villas. Our efficient system ensures fast turnaround and also quality in line with the best international standards. We offer coatings to meet the harsh challenges in today's environment.

Aluminium Aging Oven

The aging oven is used to manufacture durable, heavy duty components for long term use.

The aging process is a time and temperature dependent change in the properties of certain alloys and increases the hardness of the alloy. Heat treatment speeds up the process to achieve the desired end use properties.

our products

We have a full selection of commercial and custom profiles made of extruded aluminum alloys.

These parts can be subsequently processed by drawing and by different heat treatment modes to achieve the required mechanical values.

The extruded products can be cut to shorter lengths according to the customer's request and depointed. Further processing (machining, drilling, grinding, etc.) or surface treatment of the extrusions (anodizing and painting) can be arranged via external partners.
